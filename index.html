Lecsó recipe

Lecsó is a Hungarian dish, similar to the French ratatouille. It is usually made with egg but can be served plain or with rice.

Preparation time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients (for 2 persons):

  • 0.5-1 serving spoon of oil (or lard)
  • 1 mid-size onion
  • 2 paprikas (the light-yellow one is preferred)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Preparation:

  • Chop up the onion.
  • Cut the paprikas into rings (remove the seeds).
  • Slice the tomatoes.

Making lecsó:

  1. Heat the oil (or lard) in a frying pan.
  2. Add the chopped onion and fry it until it is golden brown.
  3. Add the paprika rings and some salt. Cover it and cook it until the paprika is soft (approximately 10 minutes).
  4. Add the sliced tomatoes and some sugar. Cook it until the the tomatoes are soft (approximately 5 minutes).
  5. Ready to serve.
