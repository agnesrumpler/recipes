Lecsó recipe

Lecsó is a Hungarian dish, similar to the French ratatouille. It is usually made with egg but can be served plain or with rice. Preparation time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients (for 2 persons):

  • 0.5 serving spoon of oil (or lard)
  • 1 mid-size onion
  • 2 TV paprikas (the Hungarian light-yellow one)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Sugar
